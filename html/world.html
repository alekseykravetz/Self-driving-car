Import from OpenStreetMap

Load real-world road data into the editor. Data sourced from overpass-turbo.eu.

How to get the data
  1. Click Open Overpass Turbo below to open the site in a new tab.
  2. Pan/zoom to your area of interest on the map, then click Run.
  3. Click Export → Copy raw OSM data.
  4. Come back here and paste the raw JSON into the textarea below.
  5. Click Import Roads.
